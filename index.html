Introducing My Self


My name is Tisha May Tizon, I'm 21 years old and currently pursuing a Bachelors Degree on Information Systems at Carlos Hilado Memorial State University.

I live in Vista Alegre, Bacolod City . My mom is a housewife and my Dad is a security officer at Airasia Philippines. I have two(2) siblings, a boy and a girl. My sister is in 5th grade and my brother is in 11th grade at STI West Negros University.

On my free time, I enjoy watching movies and reading philosophy books . However, there are also times that I would just take a nap all day long, and feel great the moment I wake up but being productive all day long also feels great to me, as if sweats were so worth it. Nevertheless, it all depends.
